Located in 1234 NW Maple St, Portland, OR 97209

Monday-Friday: 12am-9pm

Saturday & Sunday: 12pm-10pm

Yelp Reviews

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"A Vegetarian Paradise!"

I can't believe I waited so long to try The Leafy Spoon! The Vegetable Tikka Masala was rich, flavorful, and perfectly spiced, and the naan was soft and warm—just the way I like it. I also ordered the Hibiscus Iced Tea, and it was so refreshing with just the right amount of sweetness. The atmosphere was cozy, and the staff was super friendly. Definitely coming back to try the Vegetarian Paella next time!

— Samantha P.


⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Tasty, But a Bit Pricey"

I had the Spinach and Feta Spanakopita, and it was absolutely delicious—flaky, buttery, and full of flavor. My friend got the Greek Orzo Salad, and she raved about how fresh and zesty it was. The only downside is that the portions were a little small for the price, but the quality of the food was excellent.

— Jordan M.


⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Even Carnivores Will Love This Place!"

As someone who usually eats meat, I was skeptical about a fully vegetarian restaurant, but The Leafy Spoon changed my mind! The Mushroom Stroganoff was rich, creamy, and so satisfying—I didn’t even miss the meat. My partner had the Vegetarian Shepherd’s Pie, and it was warm, comforting, and packed with flavor. We ended the meal with a Matcha Latte, and it was the perfect finish. Highly recommend!

— Alex R.

Located in 5678 Sunset Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90028

Monday-Thursday: 10am-10pm

Friday-Sunday: 10am-11pm

Yelp Reviews

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Hidden Gem for Plant-Based Foodies"

If you’re looking for a vegetarian spot with gourmet flavors, this is it! I had the Falafel Bites with Tzatziki, and they were perfectly crisp on the outside and soft on the inside. The Mexican Street Corn Salad had the perfect blend of charred corn, cotija cheese, and lime. And the Spiced Apple Cider? Oh my goodness—like fall in a cup! Cozy atmosphere, friendly staff, and an incredible menu. Highly recommend!

— Emma W.


⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Delicious, But Pricey"

I loved the Baked Zucchini Fries—crispy, flavorful, and perfectly paired with marinara. The Ratatouille was beautifully plated and tasted fresh and savory. However, the prices were a bit higher than expected for a vegetarian spot. The Tropical Mango Smoothie was creamy and delicious, but for the price, I expected a larger portion. Still, the quality of the food is top-notch, so I’d come back for a special treat.

— Lily C.


⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Great Food, Slow Service"

The food at The Leafy Spoon is fantastic, but the service was a bit slow. I ordered the Mushroom Stroganoff, and it was creamy and full of flavor. The Carrot Ginger Soup had a lovely balance of sweetness and spice. However, it took nearly 30 minutes to get my food, which was a bit frustrating. If they speed up their service, this place would be perfect!

— Daniel T.
